Celebrating the Legacy of Hayti

About Us

The Hayti Heritage Center is dedicated to preserving the rich history and culture of the Hayti community. We offer a variety of programs, events, and exhibits that highlight the contributions of African Americans to our society.

Learn more

Our History

Founded in the late 19th century, the Hayti community has been a cornerstone of African American culture and heritage. The Hayti Heritage Center serves as a beacon of this history, showcasing the achievements and struggles of our ancestors.
